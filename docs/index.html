All your investments in a single sheet

muFunds lets you import your asset NAVs and other attributes from Morningstar and other data sources to your spreadsheet by using a simple function. Combined with the Google Finance function, you will be able to view all your mutual funds, stocks, ETFs, bonds, pension plans... in a single spreadsheet.
